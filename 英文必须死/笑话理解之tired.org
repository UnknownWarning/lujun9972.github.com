#+BEGIN_EXAMPLE
  Why can't a bike stand up for itself?

  Because it's two tired.
#+END_EXAMPLE

=tired= 有两种解释，一种是 =疲劳的=, 所谓 =two tired= 是 =too tired= 的谐音。

还有一种是 =装有轮胎的=, =two tired= 表示装有两个轮胎。

一句双关.
